When ordering, the customer can enter the required delivery address, which may differ from the billing address.

Das Unternehmen ist in der Schweiz zu Hause – a lot of Switzerland prägt den Alltag und die Werte – für ein Lebensgefühl, das auf der ganzen Welt zu Hause ist.

DELIVERY SERVICE

  • Home delivery to address given
  • Delivery to a NILE store listed on website
  • Delivery to a package collection point listed on website (only in Switzerland)

Method of payment

The postage indicated in the order confirmation will be charged. Accepted payment methods are Visa, Mastercard, Invoice (with credit approval only), PayPal.
Payment is made via Wirecard, an encrypted transmission system (see data protection). NILE Clothing AG will deliver goods only after payment. By placing an order, the customer gives permission for a credit check to be conducted. NILE Clothing AG may cancel or suspend an order if a customer is not creditworthy. NILE Clothing AG does not store customer credit card details. The data stored by NILE Clothing AG is used only as evidence of the transaction between the customer and NILE Clothing AG. Financial data is stored at Wirecard. On receipt of payment, the customer is assured that the article will be delivered reliably and promptly.

Price

Switzerland, Liechtenstein, Canada, Israel | Swiss francs (CHF) incl. VAT European Union | Euro (EUR) incl. VAT Shipping costs are not included and are charged separately. Payment for all orders is accepted only in Swiss francs or euros.

NILE Clothing AG reserves the right to change prices at any time. For products ordered by customers, prices on the date of purchase and specified on the order confirmation apply, unless there is an obvious error.

Confirmation

Each order or amendment to an order will be confirmed to the client by email. By placing an order, the customer agrees with the current GTC and the stated prices. A contractual relationship takes effect only on NILE Clothing AG’s acceptance of the customer’s online order.

Exchanges / Returns

It is the customer’s responsibility to examine goods immediately on delivery and to report any defects or discrepancies in the order. Any such defective goods, or those that do not fit the customer or are not suitable, can be returned within 14 calendar days without providing a reason. The 14 calendar days are counted from the date on which the customer receives the package and the date of the postmark on the returned package. The item must be returned in its original packaging and with the delivery note enclosed. Only unworn, undamaged and clean, complete goods with labels in the enclosed package are taken back. Shipping costs for the return of goods are borne by the customer. On receipt of goods, NILE clothing AG checks the returned items and decides whether a refund can be made. The refund will be made using the same payment method as the original payment and usually within 10 working days. Only the value of the goods will be refunded. Any shipping charges incurred will not be credited.
