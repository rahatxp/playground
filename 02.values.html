Environmental and social responsibility

NILE’s corporate philosophy puts people at the centre – everywhere and always. We take our social and environmental responsibilities seriously and consciously determine the way we treat each other and our environment. Respect, humility and perseverance are important and build confidence in the brand, the products and the company. Our most valuable assets are our employees and partners. Together, we work in a forward-looking and success-oriented way for long-term and sustainable business development.

Environmental and social responsibility – our approach

Production

We want to be economically successful in producing NILE clothing – our corporate values and responsibility to society and the environment provide us with the framework for doing so. We produce our wares exclusively in China and have no other production facilities abroad. To personally assume a major share of the responsibility as a business, we have operated a company office in Shanghai with five full-time employees and a NILE code of conduct since November 2012. One of the most important aims of the office is to ensure that our social standards are met. Co-CEO Kevin Willy, who spent the majority of his time living in Shanghai from November 2012 to April 2014, was responsible for setting up the Shanghai office.

On average, he spent two days per week in the factories of the NILE producers and can therefore personally vouch for the production process. This on-site presence will be continued by the Board of Management and the Shanghai office. This is the only way for us to maintain personal contact with the producers, implement the code of conduct and monitor production.

We are a long-standing member of BSCI and, like the major producers, are responsible for adhering to its standards and carrying out BSCI audits. In spite of the criticism voiced by some, we are unshakable in our belief that BSCI is a good organization that works to improve social standards. We pursue a strategy of close, long-term cooperation with our producers. That's why approximately 90% of NILE clothing comes from producers we have worked with for over two years, and 40% from companies we have worked with for over five years. What's more, we only have nine producers, meaning that we can visit each factory every two weeks. This enables us to trace every single item of clothing back to a particular factory. In geographical terms, we focus on the towns surrounding Shanghai and Hong Kong. In addition to the BSCI certificate, many of our producers have been awarded other certification such as WRAP, Oekotex 100 and ISO 8000. We end business relations with any producers who fail to show progress in external audits. NILE Clothing AG integrates the BSCI Code of Conduct into all manufacturing contracts and demands compliance when selecting partners.

Printed matter

NILE voluntarily offsets CO2 emissions through myclimate, which implements climate protection measures on our behalf. The significant contribution we make goes towards a project for clean energy in China – more information is available at myclimate, tracking box number 01-14-502895. NILE produces all printed material in Switzerland – from catalogues to invitations and business cards.

Collaboration with workshops

NILE supports craftsmanship in various areas and awards contracts whenever possible to studios that employ people who need a safe environment in which to work. One of our most important partners is Passage in Port and Foyer Schöni in Biel, a studio that has been indispensable in implementing many of our projects.

Post pro clima

NILE sends all postal packages through „Post Pro Clima“ and pays a premium for each package, which is invested fully in selected climate protection projects or quality emission certificates by the Post. CO2 emissions are offset completely, enabling NILE to offer carbon-neutral shipping. Every year NILE Clothing AG receives a certificate from „Pro Post Clima“ in regard to carbon-neutral shipping with the amount of CO2 offset.

TRANSPORT IN SWITZERLAND

Dürig Textillogistik & Transporte GmbH is our long-term partner for larger deliveries not covered by regular mail. Three vehicles, all of which comply with ERUR4 standards, are almost fully in use for NILE. All trips are optimally coordinated with deliveries and returns in order to avoid empty runs. This way, we can keep mileage and CO2 emissions to a minimum.
